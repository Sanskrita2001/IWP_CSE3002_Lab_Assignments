Ashok Hall Girl's School

About Us

Founded in 1951 with a handful of girls, Ashok Hall Girls' Higher Secondary School has blossomed into one of the premier educational institutions of the country. Our school follows a curriculum that helps in preparing children for opportunities and challenges – both in and out of the classroom. The career graphs of the students from this institution across the world bring home the fact that we have been able to guide our pupils to broaden their horizons and venture out into the competitive world of today with confidence. Ashok Hall Girls’ Higher Secondary School functions primarily as a day school having classes from Lower Infant to Class XII and a morning school for few classes of XI and XII. The school is recognized by the State Government and is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi.
